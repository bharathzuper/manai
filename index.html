Project Overview

The House

Not a museum, not a show home —
a house that feels genuinely rooted in its culture
and still completely liveable today.

— Core Design Intent

An 880 sq.ft plot. G+2 built, foundation for G+3. Ground floor parking; first floor my parents’; second floor mine.

Neo-Deccan Heritage — South Indian design language, carried into a house that is still completely liveable today. Green, crafted, unafraid of ornament.
