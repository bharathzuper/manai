PHASE 6Soul layer — Tanjore, bronzes, terracotta, brass urli, plants, books.

House rules — what to avoid

  • Chrome fittings, stark white grout, cool grey tiles
  • Laminate wardrobes or MDF anywhere visible
  • Fake "carved" MDF panels — commission real teak or skip
  • Glossy reflective finishes — everything matte, oiled, or waxed
  • More than one gold/brass tone per room — pick warm brass, stick to it
  • Polyester textiles, synthetic velvets (one silk-velvet bolster ok)
  • Cool-grey Inter-style typography in any built signage
  • Wall-mounted plastic AC units visible in any room

This is a living list — edit, re-price, swap vendors freely. Every choice bends toward warmth, craft, and a floor that still feels yours in twenty years.
